# Investor-Level Product Roadmap

## Phase 1: Production Foundation (0-2 months)
- Harden auth and session security.
- Move SQLite to managed PostgreSQL.
- Introduce CI/CD, test coverage gates, staging environment.
- Launch owner earnings and booking lifecycle metrics.

## Phase 2: Revenue Engine (2-5 months)
- Integrate payment collection + owner payouts.
- Commission configuration and promo engine.
- SLA-backed cancellation and dispute workflows.
- In-app and SMS/WhatsApp booking notifications.

## Phase 3: Market Expansion (5-9 months)
- Geo-expansion with district-level supply matching.
- Dynamic pricing and utilization optimization.
- Field operations dashboard for enterprise farm clients.
- Multi-language localization for regional penetration.

## Phase 4: Platform Moat (9-15 months)
- API partner ecosystem (agri-input and logistics apps).
- Telematics integrations for live tractor status.
- ML demand forecasting and owner earnings recommendations.
- Embedded financing and insurance partnerships.
